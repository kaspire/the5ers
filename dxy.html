Why EUR dominates the index
The euro represents 19 nations and the world's second-largest economy. Its 57.6% weight means DXY is, in practice, largely an inverted EUR/USD chart.
This creates a structural bias — a rising DXY nearly always correlates with EUR weakness, and vice versa.
